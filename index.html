Hello, my name is Gabriel Ichcanziho. I'm 26 years old. I'm a mechatronics engineer with a master's in computer science. My thesis topics were related to computer vision and natural language processing. When I was finishing my Bachelor, I was developing a recognition and tracking system for identifying an intelligent pen to save all the movement for later replication in a robotic arm with SCARA topology.

.

I continue my professional preparation by studying for my master's in computer science at Tecnologico de Monterrey campus Estado de México. I was also awarded another 100% scholarship given by the CONACYT organization. My thesis topic was Xenophobica classification using eXplainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) models. To accomplish that, I'm using different Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to preprocess the text. In addition, I have experience using spaCy and NLTK in Python to make a syntactic and semantic analyses.

.

I'm a Data Scientist at Interesse, working with different Machine Learning and Deep Learning models for classifying text and developing a liveness and anti-spoofing detection system.
